inSite is AUAV's web portal for presenting our 3D and geospatial drone data.

Some quick navigation tips:
  • Click-drag to move around
  • Middle-drag to orbit around a point
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out
  • You can also double-click anywhere to center the view and also zoom in on that location
Hopefully that is enough to get you started, and if you get stuck you can find more information in the 'Help' menu near the top-right of the screen.
